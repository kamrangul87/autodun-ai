If you drive in London, it’s worth checking your vehicle before you travel. ULEZ cameras are automated, and charges can apply even for short journeys. A quick compliance check helps you avoid surprises.

What is ULEZ?

ULEZ stands for Ultra Low Emission Zone. It is designed to reduce air pollution by discouraging higher-emission vehicles. ULEZ operates 24/7 (including weekends and public holidays) and is enforced by Transport for London (TfL).

ULEZ currently applies across all London boroughs. Always confirm your vehicle status using the official checker before travelling.

How the ULEZ checker works

The simplest way to check is with your number plate. TfL’s checker uses DVLA vehicle data (fuel type and emissions standard) to determine whether a daily ULEZ charge applies.

Official ULEZ checker
  • TfL “Check your vehicle”: https://tfl.gov.uk/modes/driving/check-your-vehicle/

Which vehicles are ULEZ compliant?

ULEZ compliance is based on emissions standards (Euro standards). As a rule of thumb:

  • Petrol: Euro 4 or newer (often ~2005+ registrations, but always check)
  • Diesel: Euro 6 or newer (often ~2015+ registrations, but always check)
  • Motorcycles: Euro 3 or newer
  • EVs: Fully electric vehicles are typically ULEZ compliant

Charges, penalties, and what happens if you don’t pay

If your vehicle is not compliant and you drive within the ULEZ, a daily charge applies. If you don’t pay, you risk receiving a penalty charge notice (PCN). The exact amounts and rules can change, so use TfL’s official pages: ULEZ information.

ULEZ vs Clean Air Zones (CAZ) in the UK

ULEZ is specific to London, but other UK cities operate Clean Air Zones. Rules vary by city and vehicle type. The UK Government maintains a central overview: Clean Air Zones (GOV.UK).

FAQs

Is my car automatically ULEZ compliant if it’s new?

Many newer vehicles are compliant, but not all. Always confirm using the official TfL checker.

Does ULEZ apply outside London?

ULEZ is London-only, but other cities run Clean Air Zones with their own rules.

Can visitors from outside London be fined?

Yes. ULEZ rules apply to all vehicles driving in the zone, regardless of where the car is registered.
