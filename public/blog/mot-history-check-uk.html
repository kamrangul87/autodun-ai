Checking MOT history is one of the safest ways to understand a vehicle’s condition in the UK. An MOT history online check shows previous failures, advisories, mileage records and expiry dates.

What is MOT history in the UK?

MOT history is the recorded timeline of a vehicle’s annual MOT tests, including pass/fail results, advisories and mileage captured at each test.

How to check MOT history online

  1. Enter the vehicle registration number.
  2. Fetch MOT history.
  3. Review expiry, mileage and prior failures.

What MOT history shows

  • MOT test results
  • Advisories
  • Mileage records
  • MOT expiry dates

Using MOT history to assess risk

When combined with mileage and vehicle age, MOT UK history can help drivers prepare for future MOT tests.

MOT history vs MOT status

MOT status shows if a vehicle is currently legal to drive. MOT history shows how the vehicle has performed over time.
