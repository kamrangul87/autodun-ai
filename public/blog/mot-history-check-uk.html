Checking MOT history is one of the safest ways to understand a vehicle’s condition in the UK. An MOT history online check — often done through a MOT check app — can reveal previous failures, recurring advisories, mileage patterns, and expiry dates.

What is MOT history in the UK?

MOT UK history is the recorded timeline of a vehicle’s MOT tests. A proper MOT check shows pass or fail results, advisory notes, test dates, and recorded mileage at each test.

How to check MOT history online

  1. Enter the vehicle registration number.
  2. Run the MOT history check.
  3. Review expiry date, mileage and advisory patterns.
  4. Compare results year by year.

What MOT history shows (and what it doesn’t)

  • MOT pass or fail results
  • Advisories and repeat issues
  • Mileage recorded at each test
  • MOT expiry status

MOT history does not show accident history, insurance claims, or ownership records. Combine it with other checks when buying a used car.
