Checking MOT history is one of the safest ways to understand a vehicle’s condition in the UK. An MOT history online check — often done through an MOT check app — can reveal previous failures, recurring advisories, mileage patterns and expiry dates.

What is MOT history in the UK?

MOT UK history is the recorded timeline of a vehicle’s MOT tests. A proper MOT check shows pass/fail outcomes, advisory notes, test dates and recorded mileage at each test.

How to check MOT history online

  1. Enter the vehicle registration number.
  2. Select Fetch MOT history.
  3. Review expiry, mileage and prior failures.
  4. Compare advisory patterns across years.

What MOT history shows (and what it doesn’t)

  • MOT pass/fail results
  • Advisory notes
  • Mileage recorded at test time
  • MOT expiry timeline

An MOT check app does not show accident history, finance, or ownership changes. Those require separate checks.

Using MOT history to assess risk

When MOT history is combined with mileage and age, it can help drivers plan maintenance — but it does not replace inspections.

MOT history vs MOT status

MOT status confirms if a vehicle is currently legal to drive. MOT history shows how it has performed over time. For a quick status-only check, see the Autodun MOT check app.
