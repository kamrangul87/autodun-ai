What the Official Guidance Says

MOT testing in the UK is governed by the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA). The official MOT inspection manual and testing standards are published at gov.uk/guidance/mot-testing-guide. This is the authoritative source for what examiners check and how faults are categorised as dangerous, major, or minor.

For EV infrastructure and zero-emission vehicles, the Office for Zero Emission Vehicles publishes policy guidance and charging network data at gov.uk/government/organisations/office-for-zero-emission-vehicles. Autodun's tools complement these official resources — they don't replace them, and any regulatory or legal question should always be verified with the relevant official UK body.
